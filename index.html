Chapter 01 · Mar 2016 - Jun 2017 · GIS Analyst, HERE Technologies

Where shipping beat proposing.

My first corporate job was enriching Western Europe maps point by point. Then Pokémon Go came out, and its geo-tagged capture mechanic looked like the answer to our slowest workflow. I proposed an in-house field-capture app, led the operations side, and shipped it with engineering: capture rate up from 30 to 65 POIs an hour at 99.93% accuracy, a 15-month plan done in 8.5. HERE gave it the Golden Impulse Award for Process and Product Excellence.

Chapter 02 · May 2019 - Jul 2022 · Manager, Digital Payments Operations (OSG)

Learning money where it breaks.

Three years in the engine room of Kotak's acquiring and payment-gateway business - merchant reporting, settlements, reconciliation, then end-to-end ownership of ecom chargebacks and representation. Automated the daily merchant reporting workflow from 9 hours to 1.5 at 99.98% accuracy, ran three channels with a team of three through COVID, and ended in Operation Intelligence, where fee dashboards surfaced ~INR 19 lakh a year in pricing anomalies. Promoted from Deputy Manager in 2021.

Chapter 03 · Jul 2022 - Nov 2025 · Product Manager, NPCI Bharat Billpay (NBBL)

Population scale.

The regulator-adjacent side of India's bill-payment rails.
